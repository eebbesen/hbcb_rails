Bibye Lake Jr., Governor, Hudson's Bay Company, 1770-1782, was the son of Sir Bibye Lake, first Baronet. He married Anne, daughter of Henry

Sperling of Dyneshall, County Essex. He was appointed a member of the Committee in 17 November, 1743; Deputy Governor 21 November, 1765

and Governor 1 February 1770. He died in office on 26 July 1782, at his house in Hertfordshire. At his death he is described as late of the Parish of

St. James, Westminster. His Goods Chattels and Credits were left to Anna Marie Lake, his Spinster daughter.
