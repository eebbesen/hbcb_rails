REID, FRANCIS/2

Excerpt from The Beaver, September 1929, p. 285:

"Frank Reid, post manager, Isle a la Crosse, and W. C. Rothnie, post manager, Pelican Narrows, joined the throng

of married employees on July 20 and 25 respectively. The brides were met by the future husbands in Winnipeg

and were married a few days later. Both Mrs. Reid and Mrs. Rothnie are new arrivals in Canada from Scotland..."

Excerpt from The Beaver, June 1933, p. 61:

"Mrs. Frank Reid and family, of Green Lake post, returned during the month of March, having spent the winter in Scotland."

Operated general store "F. Reid & Sons" in Cochenour, Ontario for 20 years, retiring from that enterprise in 1968. Red Lake District News,
