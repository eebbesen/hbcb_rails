Baptiste Charette appears in the Red River Censuses of 1838, 1840 and 1843. He was listed as a Native (ie. born in Rupert's Land) and a Catholic.

In 1838 he was unmarried and living with J. Simon Belgarde. Also living with J. Belgarde was a Joseph Charette. (Native, Catholic, unmarried). In

1840 & 1843 Baptiste Charette was listed as married with one daughter under fifteen years of age. He owned 2 acres of cultivated land. In 1840

Joseph Charette was married with one son under sixteen, one daughter under fifteen and he owned 3 acres of cultivated land. (E.5/9 fos. 7d-8, E.5/10

fos. 7d-8, E.5/11 fos. 4d-5)
