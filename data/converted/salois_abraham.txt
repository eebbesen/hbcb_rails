Abraham Salois, a native of Rupert's Land joined the Hudson's Bay Company in 1848 as a middleman          in the Saskatchewan District at wages of 17

pounds sterling per annum. He served in that capacity and as an interpreter and trader until leaving the  service of the Company in 1870 to become a

freeman in the Saskatchewan District.
