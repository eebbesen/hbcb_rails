According to J.T. Walbran, British Columbia Coast Names (1909), pp. 419-420, James Murray Reid was employed

for 28 years by the Hudson's Bay Company. He left the service after the loss of the Vancouver in 1853 and settled in Victoria where he engaged in mercantile

pursuits.

Reid's dates of birth and death and place of origin are taken from Walbran.
