Mr. Joseph Wrigley was born in Yorkshire, England, in 1839, and educated at Rugby College. He was involved in the commercial life of Great

Britain having been sent on a trade mission to Russia in 1866 and to France in 1876.1

Mr. Wrigley was Trade Commissioner for the Hudson's Bay Company in Canada from 1884 to 1891. 2 After his retirement from the Company        he

lived in London, England, and died in 1926. 3
