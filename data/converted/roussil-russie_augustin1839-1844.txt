Russie, Augustin, Jr. (Augustin Paul) son of Augustin Sr., and Rose Chinook and half sister to Catherine Russie Chalifoux.

He married Agnes (Anne Norwest) Tyikwari in 1844. Father of Joseph, Paul Marie (died at 8 mo.) and Marquerite. A son

Hyacinthe was born posthumously two weeks after Augustin's death. Agnes Russie, widow, married William Tison. Catholic
