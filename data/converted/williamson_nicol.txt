Excerpt from 1924 Annual Report for the Lake Superior District, dated October 15, 1924:                                  A.74/53, p.307

"Missanabie. Nicol Williamson, clerk, age 25 years, 6 years' service. Single. Mr. Williamson is

a good bookkeeper, and was able to keep the Company's trade books under their system and close

out the books and accounts at the end of the Outfit. Was not very suitable as a store clerk, and a

very poor stock man. Salary $5.00 per month; no overdraft for Outfit."

Excerpt from The Beaver, June 1929, p. 244:

"Nick Williamson, formerly of Red Lake and Missababie posts, was reinstated and given the position of bookkeeper         in place of
