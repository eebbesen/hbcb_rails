Sir Atwell Lake, 2nd Baronet, Governor of the Hudson's Bay Company; Governor of Hudson's Bay Company 1750-1760; baptised 9th May, 1713,

married July, 1740. Mary, only daughter of James Winter of Mile End, and had seven children of whom 1. Mary, the eldest daughter, married Major

William Webb, and 2. Anne, the second died unmarried in 1834. She died 19th April, 1782. He died 10th April, 1760. He was succeeded by his

eldest son Sir James Winter Lake.

(Burke's "Peerage and Baronetage" ­ 1930)
