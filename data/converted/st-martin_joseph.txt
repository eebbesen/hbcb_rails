NAME: ST. MARTIN, Joseph                   PARISH: Sorel, Montreal           ENTERED SERVICE: ca. 1813        DATES: b. 1786 d. 1825

Appointments & Service

Outfit Year*:           Position:                                     Post:  District:                        HBCA Reference:

*An Outfit year ran from 1 June to 31 May

Joseph St. Martin was employed at Fort George by the North West Company during the winter of 1813-14 until his engagement ended in 1815.

(H.B.C. Archives F.4/61, fo.7d)

Due to lack of records his career with the Hudson's Bay Company cannot be traced until 1821. He served the Company in the Columbia Department

as a middleman. His parish is listed as Sorel and his age as 38 years in 1824. He drowned in September 1825.

(H.B.C. Archives B.239/g/1, fo.76; B.239/g/4, fo.42; B.239/g/5, fo.41)
