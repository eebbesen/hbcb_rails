*An Outfit year ran from 1 June to 31 May

William Stanger entered the Company's service in 1850 and sailed to York Factory that same year aboard the ship Prince of Wales. He arrived at

York Factory on August 12, (HBC Archives C 1/836, fo.27d)

Throughout his five years with the Company, William Stanger served as a Labourer in the English River District (in the province of

Saskatchewan), (HBC archives B 239/g/30 ­ 34). Upon the termination of his contract in 1855 he returned to England once again aboard the Prince

of Wales. (HBC Archives B 239/g/35, fo.52; C 1/846, fo.2d)
