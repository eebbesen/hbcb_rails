Frank C. Dickson was hired in Winnipeg by the Hudson's Bay Company on 16 September 1889 as a Clerk. (HBC Archives, Hudson's Bay Company

Clerks and Postmasters, 1889-1893, Unclassified). He served at Lake Winnipeg until 1891 and at Lower Fort Garry until 1 June 1893 when his

contract was cancelled and he was re-engaged at the Saleshop on a monthly basis (HBC Archives, Fur Trade Engagement Register to 1892, Fur

Trade Engagement Register 1892-1903, Unclassified).
