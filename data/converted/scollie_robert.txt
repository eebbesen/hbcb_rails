*An Outfit year ran from 1 June to 31 May

Scollie (Scolley) was engaged by the Company as a Labourer in 1818. At this time he is noted as being from the Parish of Westray in the Orkney

Islands. In 1829 he is recorded as being 30 years of age. (HBC Archives A.30/16, fo. 60; B.135/g/10, fo. 11).

He served the Company as Bowsman, Middleman and Carpenter in the Albany River District until 1836, at which time he returned home. (HBC

Archives B.135/g/11-19).
