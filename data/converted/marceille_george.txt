The only George Marceille, employee, traced in the area, is shown as aged 17, an apprentice labourer, from the Indian Country in 1857-58.      This was

his first year in the Company's employ and he was stationed in the Rupert's River District (H.B.C. Arch. B/135/g/41, fo.10);

Retired from Albany to Montreal before out fit 1868 (B.135/g/51)
