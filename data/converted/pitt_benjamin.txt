Pitt, Benjamin, Governor, Hudson's Bay Company, 1743-46.

Benjamin Pitt was elected a member of the Committee of the Hudson's Bay Company 27 November 1711; Deputy Governor, 6 November 1735, and

Governor 17 November 1743. He attended his last meeting on 20 December 1745. In the minutes for 9 and 15 January 1746 he is reported as being

ill, he died 27 March 1746. He was married and had one son, Henry. (Search file)
