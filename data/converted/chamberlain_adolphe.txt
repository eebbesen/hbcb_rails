Warner & Munnick, Catholic Church Records of the Pacific Northwest. Vancouver

Volumes I and II and Stellamaris Mission (French Prairie Press, 1972):

According to the biographical note on pages A-13 & A-14, Chamberlain did not retire to Canada but to French              Prairie.

His first wife was Julienne, the daughter of George Watièce, by whom he had five recorded children.

His second wife was Louise Humpherville, by whom he had ll children, born between 1851 and 1869.

The same volume lists the marriage of Adolphe and Julienne on 27 Dec. 1838 (she was baptized the same day,               aged 18)

and the baptisms of their children, Zoé (26 June 1839) and Joseph (4 Oct. 1840).
