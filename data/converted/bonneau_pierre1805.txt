Pierre Bonneau is first traced in the census records in 1829. In that year he was listed as twenty-three years of age, a native of Ruperts Land, Roman

Catholic and married.

(HBC Archives, E.5/3,fo.14d)

The last entry traced to Pierre Bonneau in 1838 lists him as thirty-three years of age, married, with two sons and one daughter residing with him.

(HBC Archives, E.5/9,fo.32d)
