Ulphi Cossette Entered service about 1862 in capacity of Midman. Listed as being from Parish of Trois   Rivieres. Served in the Mackenzie River

District from 1862-1865. From this date until 1868 he is listed in Supplementary Accounts of Abstracts  of Servants Account. After 1869 he is not

listed.
