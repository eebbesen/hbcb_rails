Waswanipi Post Journal (B.227/a/46 fo. 18d) states that Thomas Beads died on a journey from Waswanipi                   to  Migiskan, March    10, 1870. He died of a

stomach complaint that had attacked him the previous April. The journal states that, "This is a severe loss             to  his poor lame son  and widow who I fear is  left  in

a state of great destitution (sic) may Good (sic) be their help & comfort under their severe bereavement".
