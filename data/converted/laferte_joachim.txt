Extract from Catholic Church Records of the Pacific Northwest, Vancouver I & II, & Stellamaris Mission (A-45)reads: Joseph [also listed as Joachim]

Laferte (the name gradually became Lafferty) was born in Canada in 1813. He settled his claim at Butteville in 1847 and beame a naturalized citizen in

1851. From his marriage to Sophie Aubichon in 1845, several children were born, but all preceded their mother in death. Sophie died in March, 1905, at

the age of seventy-seven.
