Parents: Basile Lapointe-Desautels + Catherine Henry

Father: died when he was 2½ years old. Stepfather was Jean Baptiste Rivet.

Married Lucie Laporte, daughter of Toussant and Marguerite Piche on 7 Jan.  1823 at L'Assomption.

Buried at St. Paul de Joliette, Joliette, Quebec - 23 Aug. 1873.

Joined NWCo. in May 1812. Was a Canoeman in 1816 as he is mentioned in "The Trial of Reinhard".

Served with Writ on 5 July 1817 to be present in Montreal at the Trial of Reinhard and Mainville. Left

for Montreal in Sept. 1817, attended the Trial in Montreal and in Quebec in 1818.

Source: Morice, A.G.,   Dictionnaire historique des Canadiens et des Metis de L'Ouest.
