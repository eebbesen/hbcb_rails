John Hope, a native of Rupert's Land, joined the Hudson's Bay Company       in 1867 as a middleman in the Mackenzie River District. He  remained

there in that capacity until 1871 when he left the service of the Company.  (HBC Archives B.239/g/44, fo.36d; B.239/g/48, fo.51).
