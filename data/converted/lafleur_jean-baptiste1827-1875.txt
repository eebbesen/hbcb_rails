*An Outfit year ran from 1 June to 31 May

Jean Baptiste Lafleur joined the Hudson's Bay Company in the Athabasca District in the capacity of interpreter in 1827. When his contract with  the

Company expired in 1830 he signed a new agreement on May 1 of that year. His age stated at fourty-one (41) on that agreement. He served as

interpreter in the Athabasca District for many years. The last reference traced to him is in 1875.

(HBC Archives B.239/g/7, fo.16; A.32/37, fo.89; B.235/g/3, fo.36).
