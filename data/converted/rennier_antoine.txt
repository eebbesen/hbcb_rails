*An Outfit year ran from 1 June to 31 May

Antoine Rennier joined the Hudson's Bay Company in 1841 as a middleman in the Saskatchewan District at wages of 17 pounds sterling per       annum.

In 1847 he was promoted to interpreter in that district. He apparently left the Company in 1849 and re-joined in 1852. He again served as a

middleman and an interpreter until 1857 at which time he became a freeman in the Saskatchewan District.
